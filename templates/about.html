About This Project

📌 My Mission

To empower pilgrims and authorities with data-driven insights that improve crowd navigation, safety, and comfort during Hajj and Umrah.

🌙 Inspired by a Personal Experience

On the 27th night of Ramadan, I performed Umrah with over 4.2 million pilgrims. The spiritual beauty was profound—but the overwhelming crowd also revealed critical gaps in navigation and safety. That moment inspired this platform.

📊 What This Website Provides

  • 📍 A congestion heatmap to identify high-density zones
  • 📈 Visual insights into incidents, satisfaction, stress, and queue times
  • 🧠 A short quiz to understand your crowd behavior personality with LIVE DATA displayed

🔮 Looking Ahead

While current data is recent, the next step is integrating real-time feeds to dynamically guide pilgrims during peak seasons.
